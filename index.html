About COVID-19

What is COVID-19(Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and diseas re unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Coronaviruses are large family of viruses which may cause iness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to couse respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome MESS and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus didease COVID-19.

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze

Avoid close contact (1 meter 3 feet) with people who are unwell

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell

Stay informed by watching news & follow the recommended practices

If you have fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical care early
