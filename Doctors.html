Research

Mayo Clinic researchers continue to study the origin, causes
and transmission of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19),
as well as potential treatments and vaccines.

Find out more about COVID-19 research and clinical trials at Mayo Clinic.


Research includes:

  • Treatments such as monoclonal antibody medicines and
    convalescent plasma therapy.
  • Tests to diagnose COVID-19.
  • Tests to reveal if someone has developed antibodies to COVID-19.
  • COVID-19 vaccines.
  • Post- COVID-19.research.
  • Remote monitoring of COVID-19.symptoms.
  • COVID-19 biobank.
  • COVID-19 off-label treatment registry.
